Exquisite Corpse

When I received this assignment, I found it really interesting—it was another opportunity to draw, and I love drawing in p5.js. Shawki drew a blue Venom character for us, Jovana created a character that looked like something from a fairy tale—using different shades of the same color to depict different parts of the body—and Diana drew a very exaggerated, broad-shouldered figure. I decided to combine the most striking parts of each of their drawings. First was the broad shoulders from Diana’s character, then the large head of the blue Venom character from Shawki, and lastly, the eyes from Jovana’s drawing, which left a strong impression on me. For the color scheme, I took inspiration from Jovana’s drawing, using different shades of the same color to create contrast. Under the broad shoulders, I added the claws from Shawki’s character, making the entire figure look more dynamic.
