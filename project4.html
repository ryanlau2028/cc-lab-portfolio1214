The pufferfish

When this assignment was given, the first idea that popped into my head was a pufferfish, because it involved scaling up and down. I applied the knowledge I learned from my first assignment to draw the pufferfish. However, this time I realized that while drawing a static pufferfish is easy, making all its parts move together requires setting variables for each element, which made things more complicated. Fortunately, I was able to achieve it in the end. Unfortunately, I initially wanted the pufferfish’s mouth to gradually open from a semicircle to a full circle as its body expanded, but I couldn’t find a good way to implement this, so I had to give up on that idea.
