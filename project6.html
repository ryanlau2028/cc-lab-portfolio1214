Weekday Sneezes

At the time of this assignment, I was dealing with an allergy to cold air. I’m someone with a very sensitive nose, and during allergy episodes, I sneeze constantly. So, I thought it would be interesting to record the number of sneezes I had over the course of a week. I kept a detailed record and discovered that I usually sneeze a few times right after waking up, and I also tend to sneeze when transitioning between indoor and outdoor environments due to the cold air. Therefore, most of my sneezes were concentrated in the morning and when I left for school. However, on Wednesday, I visited a friend who owns a cat, and I’m highly allergic to cats. That day became a noticeable spike in my week’s data as I sneezed a lot during that time. When coding this dataset, I decided to represent my sneezes using blue circles as a sneeze Cloud. I created buttons for each day of the week, and when a button is clicked, the sneezes for that day appear on a timeline and then gradually fade out.
