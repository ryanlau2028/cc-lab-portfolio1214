Wave Generator

While working on this assignment, I came across the word "illusion," which made me think of water ripples. In my mind, when I drink a lot of alcohol and close my eyes, I sometimes feel a sense of hallucination, and water ripples are a fitting metaphor for that sensation—waves coming one after another. So, I wanted to create my own illusion of water ripples. I decided to use some ellipses that would move upward in a layered and orderly way to achieve this effect. I drew several ellipses and offset them to create a wave-like feeling. I adjusted their initial movement speed and amplitude to make them resemble water ripples more closely. After doing all that, the visuals already looked a lot like water ripples, but it still didn’t feel illusionary enough. So, I made the transparency of each ellipse change over time. After completing the code, I felt a great sense of accomplishment because I used the same for loop to control three different variables: the size of the ellipses, their transparency, and their movement paths. This allowed me to achieve the effect I wanted with very few lines of code.
